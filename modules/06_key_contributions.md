# Module 06 — Key Contributions Summarizer

## Purpose
Identify the major conceptual and empirical contributions of the paper.

## Extraction Rules
Identify 3–6 contributions covering:
- Architectural innovations
- Methodological breakthroughs
- Training or efficiency improvements
- Empirical results and benchmarks

## Output Format
- Heading: “Key Contributions”
- Bullet list (each bullet = 1–2 sentences)
